Game Overview

Sweet Bonanza breaks away from traditional payline slots with its 6x5 reel grid and tumble feature. Instead of paylines, you win by landing 8 or more matching symbols anywhere on the reels. When wins occur, symbols disappear and new ones fall from above ‚Äì this is the cascading (tumble) mechanic that can create multiple wins from a single spin.

The Tumble feature means winning symbols disappear and new symbols fall into place. This continues until no new wins are formed, potentially creating chain reactions of wins.
